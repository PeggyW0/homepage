About Me

I am currently interning in Department MakerWorld at Shenzhen Tuozhu Technology Co.,Ltd. [Bambu Lab] as a Mechanical Design Intern. I received my MPhil degree in Computer and Information Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen), China. During my master’s studies, I conducted research on modular self-reconfiguring robots in the Freeform Robotics under the supervision of Prof. Tin Lun LAM.

I received my bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Design, Manufacturing, and Automation from Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen), China, where I completed my undergraduate thesis under the supervision of Prof. Ren Weihong, focusing on multi-object tracking.

I am currently seeking PhD positions in mechatronics and robotics.

Research

SnailBot

SnailBot is a bionic snail-inspired robot. This version of SnailBot adopts an adaptive spherical gripper structure to enhance inter-module connections and employs a rigid magnetic track as the locomotion mechanism, which also provides a fundamental guarantee for reliable connections between SnailBot modules.

Projects

CyberBonsai

This is a 4 + 3 DoF CyberBonsai inspired by network aesthetics, and it is one of the projects I worked on during my internship at Bambu Lab. The two trunk joints are driven through cable actuation, while the leaf cluster opens and closes via miniature stepper motors. I independently completed the overall structural and hardware design, and I am currently developing the control program to enable vivid, lifelike motion.
