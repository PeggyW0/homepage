    Research

    SnailBot

    SnailBot is a bionic snail-inspired robot. This version of SnailBot adopts an adaptive spherical gripper structure to enhance inter-module connections and employs a rigid magnetic track as the locomotion mechanism, which also provides a fundamental guarantee for reliable connections between SnailBot modules.

    Projects

    Adaptive Spherical Gripper

    A gripper mechanism capable of freeform connections on spherical surfaces. Designed for modular self-reconfigurable robots to enable robust and flexible connectivity.

    SnailBot Magnetic Track Locomotion

    Development of a rigid magnetic track driving system to enhance locomotion stability and connection reliability between robot modules.
